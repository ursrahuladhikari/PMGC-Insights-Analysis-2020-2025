===Format===
* '''League:''''' November 2nd - 26th, 2023 in {{FlagNoLink|my}} Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia
** '''Group Stage:''''' Nov 2nd - 19th, 2023
*** 48 teams, divided equally into 3 groups of 16.
*** Each group play exclusively in a certain week of 24 matches:
**** Each week consist of 4 matchdays.
**** Each matchday consist of 6 matches.
*** {{Bgcolortext|up|Top 3 teams of each group}} advance to ''Grand Finals''.
*** {{Bgcolortext|stayup|4th - 11th place of each group}} advance to ''Survival Stage''.
*** {{Bgcolortext|down|Bottom 5 teams of each group}} are eliminated.<div style=margin-top:10px></div>
** '''Survival Stage:''''' Nov 22nd - 24th, 2023
*** 24 teams, divided equally into 3 groups of 8.
*** Each group play 6 matches per day for a total of 18 matches across 3 days in a ''Round-Robin format''.
*** {{Bgcolortext|stayup|Top 16 teams}} advance to ''Last Chance''.
*** {{Bgcolortext|down|Bottom 8 teams}} are eliminated.<div style=margin-top:10px></div>
** '''Last Chance:''''' Nov 25th - 26th, 2023
*** 16 teams.
*** 12 matches.
*** {{Bgcolortext|up|Top 5 teams}} advance to ''Grand Finals''.
*** {{Bgcolortext|down|Bottom 11 teams}} are eliminated.
<div style=margin-top:15px></div>
* '''Grand Finals:''''' December 8th - 10th, 2023 in {{FlagNoLink|tr}} Istanbul, Türkiye
** 16 teams:
*** {{Bgcolortext|up|14 teams}} from ''League Stage
*** {{Bgcolortext|seedup|2 teams}} directly invited
** 18 matches
