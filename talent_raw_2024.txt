Format[edit]
League: October 31st - November 24th, 2024 in  Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia
Group Stage: Oct 31st - Nov 17th, 2024
48 teams, divided equally into 3 groups of 16.
Each group play exclusively in a certain week of 24 matches:
Each week consist of 4 matchdays.
Each matchday consist of 6 matches.
Top 3 teams of each group advance to Grand Finals.
4th - 11th place of each group advance to Survival Stage.
Bottom 5 teams of each group are eliminated.
Survival Stage: Nov 20th - 22nd, 2024
24 teams, divided equally into 3 groups of 8.
Each group play 6 matches per day for a total of 18 matches across 3 days in a Round-Robin format.
Top 16 teams advance to Last Chance.
Bottom 8 teams are eliminated.
Last Chance: Nov 23rd - 24th, 2024
16 teams.
12 matches.
Top 6 teams advance to Grand Finals.
Bottom 10 teams are eliminated.

Grand Finals: December 6th - 8th, 2024 in  London, United Kingdom
16 teams:
9 teams from Group Stage
6 teams from Last Chance
1 Host Region team directly invited
18 matches (6 each day).
