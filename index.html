Game Format & Maps

The rigorous path to becoming a World Champion.

👥

Group Stage

48 Teams divided into 3 Groups (Red, Green, Yellow). Top 3 from each group qualify directly to Grand Finals.

🛡️

Survival Stage

Teams ranked 4th-11th from each group. 24 teams battle for 16 spots in the Last Chance stage.

⚡

Last Chance

16 Teams from Survival Stage battle for the final 5 spots in the Grand Finals.

💎

Grand Finals

16 elite teams compete over 18 total matches to determine the PMGC World Champion.
