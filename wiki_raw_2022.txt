===Format===
* '''League:''''' November 10th - December 4th, 2022 &mdash; in {{Flag|my}} Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia
** '''Group Stage:''''' Nov 10th - 27th, 2022
*** 48 teams, divided equally into 3 groups of 16.
*** Each group exclusively play in a certain week, with each week consists of 4 matchdays.
*** Each matchday consists of 6 matches for a total of 24 matches per week.
*** {{Bgcolortext|up|Top 3 teams of each group}} advance to ''Grand Finals''.
*** {{Bgcolortext|stayup|4th - 11th place of each group}} advance to ''Survival Stage''.
*** {{Bgcolortext|down|Bottom 5 teams of each group}} are eliminated.<div style=margin-top:10px/>
** '''Survival Stage:''''' Nov 30th - Dec 2nd, 2022
*** 24 teams, divided equally into 3 groups of 8.
*** Each group play 6 matches per day for a total of 18 matches across 3 days in a ''Round-Robin format''.
*** {{Bgcolortext|stayup|Top 16 teams}} advance to ''Last Chance''.
*** {{Bgcolortext|down|Bottom 8 teams}} are eliminated.<div style=margin-top:10px/>
** '''Last Chance:''''' Dec 3rd - 4th, 2022
*** 16 teams.
*** 12 matches.
*** {{Bgcolortext|up|Top 5 teams}} advance to ''Grand Finals''.
*** {{Bgcolortext|down|Bottom 11 teams}} are eliminated.
<div style=margin-top:15px/>
* '''Grand Finals:''''' January 6th - 8th, 2023 &mdash; in {{Flag|id}} Jakarta, Indonesia
** 16 teams:
*** {{Bgcolortext|up|14 teams}} from ''League Stage
*** {{Bgcolortext|seedup|2 teams}} directly invited
** 18 matches
